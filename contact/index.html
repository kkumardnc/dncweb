Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours?

Our office is open Mon-Fri 9-5 and Sat-Sun 10-4. Trails are open during daylight hours year-round.

Is there an admission fee?

No! Demarest Nature Center is free and open to the public. Donations are always appreciated.

Are pets allowed?

Leashed pets are welcome on our trails. Please clean up after your pet and stay on marked trails.

Do you offer guided tours?

Yes! We offer regularly scheduled guided nature walks and can arrange special tours for groups. Check our events calendar or contact us to schedule.

How can I support the nature center?

You can become a member, volunteer your time, make a donation, or attend our fundraising events like the annual Oktoberfest Craft Show.
