Policies and Guidelines

Ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors

To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors while protecting our natural resources, please observe the following guidelines:

Trail Use and Safety

  • Trails are open from dawn to dusk
  • Stay on marked trails to protect sensitive habitats
  • Pets must be leashed at all times and waste must be removed
  • No motorized vehicles on trails
  • Bicycles are permitted on designated trails only

Wildlife and Plant Protection

  • Do not feed or approach wildlife
  • Do not remove, damage, or collect plants, flowers, or fungi
  • No hunting, trapping, or fishing
  • Observe wildlife from a respectful distance
  • Report injured or distressed wildlife to park staff

General Conduct

  • Pack out all trashâ€”Leave No Trace principles apply
  • No fires, smoking, or alcohol
  • Keep noise levels low to respect wildlife and other visitors
  • Groups of 10 or more should contact us in advance
  • Commercial photography requires prior authorization

Educational Programs

  • School groups must be supervised by teachers/chaperones
  • Advanced registration required for all educational programs
  • Program cancellations will be communicated via email
  • Some programs may have age restrictions or prerequisites

Monthly Board Meetings

The Demarest Nature Center Association Board of Trustees meets monthly to discuss operations, programs, and conservation initiatives.

  • Schedule: Third Tuesday of each month at 7:00 PM
  • Location: Demarest Nature Center Meeting Room
  • Public Welcome: Community members are encouraged to attend
  • Agenda: Available on our website prior to each meeting
  • To add items to the agenda or speak at a meeting, contact us at least one week in advance

Note: These policies help us maintain the preserve for future generations. Violations may result in removal from the property. For questions about our policies, please contact us.

Questions About Our Policies?

If you have questions or need clarification about any of our policies, please don't hesitate to reach out.
