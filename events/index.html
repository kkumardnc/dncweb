Upcoming Events

Join us for nature walks, workshops, seasonal celebrations, and community gatherings

Throughout the year, Demarest Nature Center hosts a variety of events that bring our community together to explore, learn, and celebrate the natural world. From guided hikes and bird watching expeditions to seasonal festivals and educational workshops, there's always something happening at the nature center.

Types of Events We Host

Guided Nature Walks

Join experienced naturalists for interpretive walks through our 55-acre preserve. Learn about local flora and fauna, seasonal changes, and the ecology of our woodlands and streams. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

  • Spring wildflower walks
  • Summer bird watching expeditions
  • Fall foliage tours
  • Winter tracking adventures

Family Programs

Fun, educational activities designed for families to enjoy together. These hands-on programs help children and adults connect with nature while creating lasting memories.

  • Children's scavenger hunts
  • Birdhouse building workshops
  • Nature crafts sessions
  • Seasonal celebrations

Educational Workshops

In-depth learning experiences on specialized topics led by experts. Perfect for adults and older students interested in expanding their knowledge of natural history and conservation.

  • Native plant lectures
  • Bird identification classes
  • Nature photography workshops
  • Conservation discussions

Seasonal Celebrations

Special events that mark the changing seasons and celebrate our connection to the natural world.

  • Maple syrup making demonstrations (late winter/early spring)
  • Spring migration bird counts
  • Summer nature camp showcases
  • Oktoberfest Craft Show (fall)

Specialized Expeditions

Unique opportunities to explore specific aspects of our natural environment with expert guides.

  • Mushroom foraging walks
  • Stream ecology studies
  • Bird counts and surveys
  • Night hikes and astronomy

Community Events

Larger gatherings that bring the entire community together to support the nature center and enjoy outdoor activities.

  • Annual Oktoberfest Craft Show
  • Trail cleanup days
  • Community trail walks
  • Fundraising events

Annual Event Highlights

Maple Syrup Making

When: Late February - Early March

Experience the traditional process of tapping maple trees and transforming sap into delicious syrup. This beloved annual tradition includes demonstrations of tree tapping, sap collection, and the boiling process. Families can observe every step and sample the final product!

This free community event is one of our most popular programs. Registration typically opens in January.

Bird Watching & Bird Counts

When: Year-round, with special counts in spring and fall

Join fellow birding enthusiasts for guided bird watching sessions throughout the year. Participate in official bird counts during spring and fall migration seasons, and help contribute valuable data to bird conservation efforts. Binoculars and field guides provided for beginners.

All skill levels welcome, from curious beginners to experienced birders.

Oktoberfest Craft Show

When: October (specific date announced each summer)

Our signature fall fundraising event features local artisans, craft vendors, food, music, and family-friendly activities. This beloved community tradition supports our educational programs and preserve maintenance. Browse unique handmade items while enjoying the beautiful fall foliage!

Vendor applications typically open in early summer. Proceeds support DNC programs.

Mushroom Foraging Walks

When: Spring and Fall

Explore the fascinating world of fungi with expert mycologists. Learn to identify edible and poisonous mushrooms, understand their ecological roles, and discover the incredible diversity of mushrooms in our preserve. These walks combine science, safety, and the thrill of discovery.

Limited capacity. Registration required. Please note: foraging is for educational purposes only.

How to Register

Online

Most events can be registered for through our website. Event listings will include registration links when available.

Email

Send registration requests to info@demarestnaturecenter.org with the event name and number of attendees.

Walk-Ins Welcome

Many of our trail walks and community events welcome walk-in participants. Check individual event listings for details.

Event Policies

  • Weather: Events may be cancelled due to severe weather. Check your email or our website for updates.
  • What to Bring: Wear comfortable walking shoes and weather-appropriate clothing. Bring water and insect repellent.
  • Accessibility: Contact us in advance if you have accessibility needs so we can accommodate you.
  • Age Requirements: Most events are family-friendly. Specific age requirements are noted in event descriptions.
  • Pets: For the safety of wildlife and other visitors, pets are not permitted at events unless specifically noted.
