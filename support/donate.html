Make a Donation

Every gift helps preserve nature and inspire environmental stewardship

Donate Now with Stripe

Your tax-deductible donation to Demarest Nature Center makes an immediate and lasting impact. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit run entirely by volunteers, every dollar you contribute goes directly toward maintaining our 55-acre preserve, providing educational programs, and protecting this natural sanctuary for future generations. No gift is too small - they all make a difference.

Ways to Give

One-Time Donation

Make a single gift of any amount to support our mission. Every contribution helps maintain trails, fund programs, and protect wildlife habitat.

Please see "Other Ways to Give" below for information on how to make your donation.

Monthly Giving

Join our Circle of Stewards with a recurring monthly gift. Sustaining donations provide reliable support that helps us plan programs and maintain our preserve year-round.

Benefits of Monthly Giving:

  • Provides steady, reliable support
  • Convenient automatic payments
  • Cancel or adjust anytime
  • Single annual tax receipt
  • Greater total impact over time
  • Special recognition as a sustaining donor

Even small monthly gifts add up to make a significant impact over time!

Contact us to set up a monthly donation plan.

Memorial & Honor Gifts

Honor a loved one or celebrate a special occasion with a gift to Demarest Nature Center. We'll send a personalized acknowledgment to the honoree or their family.

Common Occasions:

  • In memory of a loved one
  • In honor of a birthday or anniversary
  • To celebrate a graduation or achievement
  • In appreciation of a teacher or mentor

Contact us to make a memorial or honor gift.

Corporate & Matching Gifts

Many companies match employee donations, doubling or even tripling your impact! Check if your employer has a matching gift program.

How It Works:

  1. Make your personal donation to DNC
  2. Request a matching gift form from your employer
  3. Submit the form to your HR department
  4. Your company sends their matching contribution

Corporate Sponsorships: Interested in corporate partnership opportunities? Contact us to discuss custom sponsorship packages.

Planned Giving & Bequests

Create a lasting legacy by including Demarest Nature Center in your estate plans. Planned gifts ensure our preserve and programs continue for generations to come.

Planned Giving Options:

  • Bequests in your will or living trust
  • Beneficiary designations (IRA, life insurance, etc.)
  • Charitable gift annuities
  • Charitable remainder trusts
  • Gifts of appreciated stock or securities

Sample bequest language and planned giving information available upon request. Please consult with your financial and legal advisors.

Needed Items & Services:

Donor Recognition

We are grateful to all our donors and recognize your generosity in the following ways:

  • Annual Report: All donors (unless anonymous) are listed in our annual report
  • Website Recognition: Major donors ($500+) are recognized on our website
  • Newsletter Thanks: Donors are acknowledged in our quarterly newsletter
  • Wall of Honor: Lifetime members and major benefactors receive permanent recognition
  • Named Opportunities: Opportunities to name programs, trails, or facilities for significant gifts

We respect your privacy. If you prefer to remain anonymous, please indicate this preference when making your donation.

Tax Information

Demarest Nature Center is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

Legal Name: Demarest Nature Center, Inc.

Tax ID (EIN): Available upon request

Tax Receipt: You will receive an acknowledgment letter for tax purposes via email (or mail if requested)

Please consult your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of your contribution. No goods or services were provided in exchange for your donation (unless otherwise noted).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Demarest Nature Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

How will my donation be used?

Your donation directly supports our educational programs, trail maintenance, habitat conservation, and community events. As an all-volunteer organization, 100% of your gift goes to programs and preserve management.

Can I designate my gift for a specific purpose?

Yes! You can designate your gift for specific programs or projects. Please note your preference in the comments section of the donation form or contact us to discuss.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your monthly donation at any time by contacting us at info@demarestnaturecenter.org.

How do I update my payment information?

Contact us and we'll help you update your credit card or banking information for recurring donations.

Thank You

Every donation, regardless of size, makes a real difference in preserving our natural environment and inspiring the next generation of environmental stewards. Thank you for considering a gift to Demarest Nature Center.
