Make a Donation

Every gift helps preserve nature and inspire environmental stewardship

Your tax-deductible donation to Demarest Nature Center makes an immediate and lasting impact. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit run entirely by volunteers, every dollar you contribute goes directly toward maintaining our 55-acre preserve, providing educational programs, and protecting this natural sanctuary for future generations. No gift is too small - they all make a difference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Demarest Nature Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

How will my donation be used?

Your donation directly supports our educational programs, trail maintenance, habitat conservation, and community events. As an all-volunteer organization, 100% of your gift goes to programs and preserve management.

Can I designate my gift for a specific purpose?

Yes! You can designate your gift for specific programs or projects. Please note your preference in the comments section of the donation form or contact us to discuss.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your monthly donation at any time by contacting us at info@demarestnaturecenter.org.

How do I update my payment information?

Contact us and we'll help you update your credit card or banking information for recurring donations.

Thank You
