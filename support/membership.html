Become a Member

Join our community and help preserve nature for future generations

Membership at Demarest Nature Center is more than just a donation - it's joining a community of people who care deeply about environmental education and conservation. Your membership directly supports our programs, maintains our trails, and ensures that our 55-acre preserve remains a free, accessible natural sanctuary for all.

Member Perks Throughout the Year

Quarterly Newsletter

Stay informed with our members-only newsletter featuring program schedules, conservation updates, wildlife spotting reports, and volunteer opportunities.

Priority Registration

Get first access to register for popular programs like maple syrup making, summer camps, and limited-capacity workshops.

Members-Only Events

Enjoy exclusive events throughout the year including sunrise bird walks, seasonal nature talks, and our annual member appreciation celebration.

Workshop Discounts

Save on workshops and special programs throughout the year. Family members save 15% on nature photography, bird watching, and adult education workshops.

Community Connection

Join a network of like-minded individuals who share your passion for nature and environmental stewardship.

Make an Impact

Know that your support directly enables our educational programs, trail maintenance, and conservation work.

How Your Membership Helps

$35 Individual Membership

  • Provides environmental education materials for 10 students
  • Maintains 50 feet of trail for one year
  • Supplies field guides and binoculars for public use

$60 Family Membership

  • Supports one full classroom field trip
  • Funds supplies for two family programs
  • Contributes to our scholarship program

$125 Supporting Membership

  • Sponsors a week of summer nature camp
  • Funds invasive plant removal projects
  • Supports wildlife habitat improvements

$250 Patron Membership

  • Funds an entire K-4 school program series
  • Supports major trail improvements and bridge maintenance
  • Enables year-round interpretive programming

Give the Gift of Membership

A Demarest Nature Center membership makes a thoughtful, meaningful gift for nature lovers, families, and anyone who cares about the environment. Gift memberships include all member benefits plus a special gift card announcing the membership.

To purchase a gift membership, fill out the form above with the recipient's information, or contact us at info@demarestnaturecenter.org to arrange a personalized gift.

Renewing Your Membership?

Thank you for your continued support! Current members can renew online using the form above, by mail, or in person. We'll send renewal reminders 30 days before your membership expires.

Auto-renewal is available - contact us to set up automatic annual renewal and never miss a year of support.
