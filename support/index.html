Support Demarest Nature Center

Help us preserve nature and inspire environmental stewardship for generations to come

Demarest Nature Center is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization run entirely by volunteers. We receive no funding from the Borough of Demarest. Every program, every trail, and every educational experience is made possible by the generous support of people like you. Your contributions help us maintain our 55-acre preserve, provide free and low-cost educational programs, and inspire the next generation of environmental stewards.

What Your Support Funds

Educational Programs

Supporting comprehensive K-4 environmental education in Demarest schools, summer nature camps, family programs, and adult workshops. We provide curriculum materials, equipment, and expert instruction to thousands of participants each year.

Trail Maintenance & Improvement

Maintaining over 2 miles of trails, building and repairing bridges, installing benches and interpretive signs, and ensuring safe access for all visitors to explore our preserve.

Conservation & Habitat Management

Protecting native plant communities, removing invasive species, monitoring wildlife populations, and maintaining healthy ecosystems that support biodiversity.

Community Events

Hosting maple syrup making demonstrations, bird watching expeditions, nature photography workshops, children's scavenger hunts, and our annual Oktoberfest Craft Show.

Scholarship Program

Providing annual scholarships to high school seniors pursuing studies in environmental science, ecology, and conservation, supporting the next generation of environmental leaders.

Equipment & Supplies

Purchasing educational materials, trail maintenance tools, safety equipment, field guides, binoculars, and supplies needed for programs and preserve management.
