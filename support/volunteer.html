Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers making a difference in our community

Demarest Nature Center is run entirely by volunteers - from our board of directors to our trail maintenance crew to our program educators. Every hour volunteered helps us maintain our preserve, deliver educational programs, and inspire environmental stewardship. Whether you have a few hours a month or can commit to regular involvement, there's a meaningful way for you to contribute.

Volunteer Opportunities

Trail Maintenance & Stewardship

Help maintain over 2 miles of trails, clear brush, repair bridges, install benches, and keep our preserve accessible and beautiful.

Activities Include:

  • Trail clearing and maintenance
  • Bridge and boardwalk repair
  • Invasive plant removal
  • Planting native species
  • Installing and maintaining trail markers
  • General preserve cleanup

Time Commitment: Flexible - join monthly trail days or help on your own schedule

Best For: Those who enjoy outdoor physical work

Education & Program Leadership

Share your knowledge and passion for nature by leading programs, assisting with school groups, or teaching workshops.

Activities Include:

  • Leading guided nature walks
  • Assisting with school field trips
  • Teaching workshops (bird watching, plant ID, etc.)
  • Supporting summer camp programs
  • Developing educational materials
  • Conducting wildlife surveys

Time Commitment: Varies by program - from one-time events to ongoing roles

Best For: Educators, naturalists, those with specialized knowledge

Event Support

Help make our events successful by assisting with setup, registration, activities, and logistics for programs throughout the year.

Activities Include:

  • Event setup and breakdown
  • Registration and check-in
  • Activity stations at family events
  • Oktoberfest Craft Show support
  • Maple syrup making demos
  • Refreshment coordination

Time Commitment: Event-based - a few hours per event

Best For: People-oriented volunteers who enjoy community events

Administrative & Office Support

Provide behind-the-scenes support that keeps our organization running smoothly.

Activities Include:

  • Newsletter writing and editing
  • Social media management
  • Website updates
  • Membership database maintenance
  • Grant research and writing
  • Photography and videography
  • Graphic design for materials

Time Commitment: Flexible - can often be done remotely

Best For: Those with professional skills in writing, design, communications

Wildlife Monitoring & Citizen Science

Contribute to scientific research and conservation by participating in wildlife surveys and monitoring programs.

Activities Include:

  • Bird counts and surveys
  • Wildlife observation and documentation
  • Stream monitoring and water quality testing
  • Plant surveys and phenology tracking
  • Photo documentation of species
  • Data entry and analysis

Time Commitment: Varies - from single survey days to ongoing monitoring

Best For: Nature observers, photographers, data enthusiasts

Board & Committee Service

Provide leadership and strategic direction as a board member or committee participant.

Opportunities Include:

  • Board of Directors positions
  • Fundraising committee
  • Education program committee
  • Facilities and grounds committee
  • Marketing and outreach committee
  • Special project teams

Time Commitment: Regular meetings plus additional work

Best For: Those with governance, nonprofit, or specialized professional experience

Benefits of Volunteering

Make a Real Impact

Your work directly supports environmental education and conservation in our community. See the tangible results of your efforts.

Learn & Grow

Develop new skills, deepen your knowledge of nature, and gain experience in nonprofit work, education, or conservation.

Connect with Community

Meet like-minded people who share your passion for nature and environmental stewardship. Build lasting friendships.

Enjoy the Outdoors

Spend time in nature while doing meaningful work. Experience the preserve in all seasons and discover its hidden treasures.

Flexible Opportunities

Choose commitments that fit your schedule and interests. One-time, occasional, or regular opportunities available.

Recognition & Appreciation

Volunteers are honored at our annual appreciation event and recognized in our newsletter and annual report.
