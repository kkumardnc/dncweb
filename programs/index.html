Our Programs

Connecting people with nature through education and hands-on experiences

At Demarest Nature Center, we offer a wide range of programs designed to inspire environmental awareness and foster a deep connection with the natural world. From school-aged children to adults, our programs provide meaningful outdoor experiences that educate, engage, and empower participants to become stewards of the environment.

School Programs

Our curriculum-aligned outdoor education programs bring students from kindergarten through 12th grade into direct contact with nature. We partner with local schools to provide hands-on learning experiences that complement classroom instruction and meet educational standards.

Program Features:

  • Standards-aligned environmental curriculum for K-12
  • Guided nature walks and field investigations
  • Seasonal ecology studies
  • Wildlife observation and habitat exploration
  • Stream ecology and water quality testing
  • Tree and plant identification
  • Environmental science experiments
  • Conservation education workshops

Educational Impact:

We proudly support comprehensive K-4 environmental education programs in Demarest schools, providing thousands of students with meaningful outdoor learning experiences each year. Our programs help students develop critical thinking skills, scientific inquiry abilities, and a lifelong appreciation for nature.

Schedule a School Visit

Summer Camps

Our nature-based day camps provide children ages 5-12 with unforgettable summer experiences. Campers explore the trails, discover wildlife, engage in hands-on nature activities, and make lasting friendships while developing a deeper connection to the natural world.

Camp Activities Include:

  • Trail explorations and nature hikes
  • Wildlife observation and tracking
  • Stream studies and aquatic life discovery
  • Nature crafts and outdoor art
  • Team-building games and activities
  • Environmental education lessons
  • Scavenger hunts and nature challenges
  • Storytelling and outdoor adventures

Camp Details:

Ages: 5-12 years old
Format: Day camps (no overnight)
Schedule: Multiple weekly sessions throughout summer
Activities: Age-appropriate outdoor learning and play

Register for Summer Camp

Family Programs

Our family programs are designed to bring multiple generations together to explore and appreciate nature. These guided experiences provide opportunities for families to learn together, create memories, and deepen their connection to the natural world.

Popular Family Activities:

  • Guided nature hikes for all ages and abilities
  • Seasonal celebrations (spring wildflowers, fall foliage, winter tracking)
  • Maple syrup making demonstrations
  • Children's scavenger hunts
  • Birdhouse and bird feeder building workshops
  • Nature photography walks
  • Mushroom foraging walks with experts
  • Community trail walks
  • Holiday nature crafts

Why Families Love Our Programs:

Our family programs are inclusive, educational, and fun! Whether you're experienced hikers or first-time nature explorers, our knowledgeable volunteers create welcoming environments where everyone can learn and enjoy the outdoors together.

View Upcoming Family Events

Adult Workshops

Our adult workshops cater to nature enthusiasts who want to deepen their knowledge and skills. Led by experts and experienced naturalists, these programs cover a wide range of topics from bird watching to conservation practices.

Workshop Topics Include:

  • Bird Watching: Learn identification skills, birding by ear, and participate in bird counts
  • Nature Photography: Capture the beauty of the natural world with composition and technique workshops
  • Native Plant Gardening: Discover how to create wildlife-friendly landscapes with native species
  • Conservation Practices: Learn about sustainable living and environmental stewardship
  • Ecology Lectures: Expert presentations on local ecology, wildlife, and environmental topics
  • Trail Maintenance: Hands-on workshops on trail care and land management
  • Mushroom Identification: Guided foraging walks with mycology experts
  • Seasonal Nature Study: In-depth explorations of seasonal changes in our ecosystem

Expert-Led Learning:

Our workshops are taught by professional naturalists, master gardeners, conservation experts, and passionate volunteers who bring decades of combined experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced naturalist, you'll find programs that challenge and inspire you.

High School Scholarship Program

Each year, Demarest Nature Center awards an annual scholarship to a high school senior pursuing studies in environmental science, ecology, conservation, or related fields. This scholarship supports the next generation of environmental leaders and reflects our commitment to education.

Eligibility: High school seniors planning to study environmental or natural sciences

Supporting the Community

In addition to public and school events and the development and maintenance of the Nature Center grounds, the Association provides:

  • Subscriptions of nature and environmentally oriented publications to the Demarest Public Library
  • Occasional grants to the library for purchasing environmental publications and videos
  • Scholarship awards to graduating seniors interested in environmental sciences
