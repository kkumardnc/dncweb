John C. Goodwin Nature Photo Contest

Annual nature photography competition

About the Contest

The John C. Goodwin Nature Photo Contest celebrates the beauty of nature captured at the Demarest Nature Center. Named in honor of John C. Goodwin, a passionate nature photographer and supporter of the Center, this annual competition invites photographers of all skill levels to submit their best nature photography taken within the preserve.
