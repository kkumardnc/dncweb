Welcome To The Demarest Nature Center

The Demarest Nature Center is a beautiful natural sanctuary dedicated to connecting people with nature through education, conservation, and outdoor experiences. Our trails, programs, and facilities provide opportunities for visitors of all ages to explore, learn, and appreciate the natural world.

Whether you're interested in hiking our scenic trails, participating in educational programs, attending seasonal events, or simply enjoying the peace and beauty of nature, we welcome you to visit and discover all that the Demarest Nature Center has to offer.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire environmental stewardship and foster a deep connection with nature through engaging educational programs, conservation efforts, and accessible outdoor experiences for our community.

We believe that by connecting people with the natural world, we can cultivate a generation of environmental advocates who will protect and preserve our precious natural resources for future generations.
