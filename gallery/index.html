Annual Photo Contest

Each year, we host a nature photography contest open to all ages. Photographers are invited to capture the beauty of Demarest Nature Center through the seasons. Winners are displayed in our visitor center and featured on our website.

Categories Include:

  • Wildlife Photography
  • Landscape & Trail Scenes
  • Macro Photography (plants, insects, details)
  • Seasonal Beauty
  • Youth Photography (ages 17 and under)

Contest Timeline: Submissions typically accepted in late summer/early fall. Winners announced at our Oktoberfest Craft Show.
